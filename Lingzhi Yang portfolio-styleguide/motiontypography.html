Project 02

Transformers Motion Typography

A motion graphics video created in After Effects, inspired by Optimus Prime’s monologue and developed through text animation, cinematic pacing, and visual rhythm.

After Effects Motion Design Typography Video School Project

Overview

Project Overview

This project is a dynamic text animation inspired by one of Optimus Prime’s most iconic lines from Transformers. I used the voice and script as the foundation for building a visually dramatic motion piece centered on animated typography.

The goal was to create a video that could communicate emotion, narrative, and cinematic energy through text rather than through characters or traditional scene-based animation.

Role

My Contribution

I was responsible for the full concept, visual direction, motion design, text animation, pacing, and final editing of the project.

I used After Effects as the primary tool, while Premiere Pro, Photoshop, PowerPoint, and Meitu supported visual adjustments and editing during the development process.

Process

Design and Development Process

I began by selecting the script and breaking the monologue into smaller sections so I could control pacing and emphasis more carefully. This helped me understand where visual tension, pause, and movement should appear in the animation.

After that, I started experimenting in After Effects with animated text, scale changes, transitions, and timing. My first version felt visually too simple, so I continued researching motion graphics techniques and explored ways to create stronger visual rhythm and impact.

In later stages, I refined the overall composition and improved the relationship between typography and pacing. I focused on making the words feel expressive and cinematic, rather than simply readable.

Outcome

Reflection

This project helped me strengthen my understanding of motion design, especially the role of typography, pacing, and timing in shaping emotional tone. It also showed me how text alone can become a powerful storytelling tool when combined with animation and careful visual rhythm.
